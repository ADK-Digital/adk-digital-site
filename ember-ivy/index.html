About Ember & Ivy

An intimate story of fire and flavor

Born from a love of communal fire and the flavors it inspires, Ember & Ivy blends rustic techniques with modern finesse. Our chefs coax smoke and embers into every dish and drink, crafting a menu that’s as seasonal as it is soulful. From wood-fired mains to charred citrus cocktails, every plate tells a story of warmth and craft.

We source locally and sustainably, partnering with regional farms and artisans to celebrate the bounty of the Northeast. Whether you’re joining us for an intimate date or a lively gathering, expect cozy corners, attentive hospitality and a space designed to linger long after the last spark fades.
