About Us

Keystone & Beam is a detail-driven residential contractor focused on high-end finishes and thoughtful construction. From log-cabin interiors and stone fireplaces to modern kitchens, lighting and outdoor living, our work blends craftsmanship with practical engineering.

We manage projects start-to-finish—planning, permitting, scheduling and trade coordination—so you enjoy a smooth process and a space that feels cohesive and built to last.
