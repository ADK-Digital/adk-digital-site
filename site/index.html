How we turn every leaf

We begin with a consultation to understand your objectives, constraints and timeline. Expect lots of questions as we work to see the full picture.

  • Discuss goals & concerns
  • Identify documents & stakeholders
  • Outline initial strategy

  • Gather documents & data
  • Identify witnesses & experts
  • Preserve electronic evidence

  • Case law & precedent research
  • Strength/weakness analysis

  • Detailed action plan

  • Regular status reports

Representative matters

Small business contract dispute

Challenge: Our client faced a breach of contract claim from a vendor seeking significant damages.

Approach: We reconstructed timelines, conducted a document review and deposed key witnesses. Leveraging meticulous discovery, we built leverage for early resolution.

Outcome: Case settled on favorable terms within three months, preserving the business relationship and avoiding trial.

Past results do not guarantee future outcomes; each matter is unique.

Employment discrimination lawsuit

Challenge: An employee alleged discrimination and sought reinstatement and back pay.

Approach: We investigated internal policies, interviewed witnesses and demonstrated compliance with applicable laws. We also pursued alternative dispute resolution to manage costs.

Outcome: The complaint was withdrawn following mediation; our client implemented updated policies to prevent future issues.

These summaries are for illustrative purposes only and do not create an attorney‑client relationship.

Startup intellectual property defense

Challenge: A competitor accused our client of software copyright infringement, threatening a product launch.

Approach: We conducted a code audit, obtained expert opinions and filed a declaratory judgment action to clarify rights.

Outcome: The court declared no infringement; our client launched on schedule with confidence and strengthened IP protocols.

Outcomes vary; these examples are not a promise of similar results.
